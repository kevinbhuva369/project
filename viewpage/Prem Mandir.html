The Sacred Legacy

Prem Mandir the Temple of Divine Love is a breathtaking white marble masterpiece built by Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj and inaugurated in 2012. Situated in the sacred land of Vrindavan, it stands as a supreme symbol of pure devotion and eternal love between the divine couple Radha-Krishna. The temple took over 11 years and 1,000 craftsmen to complete.

The temple complex spans 54 acres and is covered in intricate Italian white marble carvings depicting the love stories of Radha-Krishna and Sita-Ram. After sunset, the entire temple is illuminated in changing coloured lights saffron, blue, gold, and white creating an otherworldly, ethereal atmosphere that draws thousands of visitors every evening.

Sacred Architecture

The architecture blends ancient Rajput and Mughal temple styles with 94 ornately carved pillars. The main sanctum features life-size panels depicting the Raas Leela of Radha-Krishna. The outer walls are adorned with 84 scenes from Krishna's life in Vrindavan, carved in relief on pure white Italian marble with extraordinary detail.

Festivals & Celebrations

Radha Ashtami (Radha's birthday) and Janmashtami are celebrated with extraordinary devotion over 50,000 devotees gather for a spectacular light-and-music show with processions of the divine idols. The Holi celebrations here are legendary, with coloured flowers and divine music filling the entire 54-acre complex.

Divine Significance

Prem Mandir is not just a temple it is a living monument to the philosophy of Prem (love) as the highest path to God. Jagadguru Kripalu Ji Maharaj built this temple to demonstrate that pure love for the divine is accessible to every human being, regardless of caste, creed, or background.

Daily Rituals

Daily rituals begin at 5:30 AM with Mangala Aarti. Multiple Aarti ceremonies follow throughout the day. The evening illumination begins at 7:30 PM and the final Shayana Aarti at 8:30 PM is particularly mesmerising, accompanied by bhajan singing that fills the entire marble complex.

The divine love between Radha and Krishna is not just a story it is the cosmic secret of how the finite soul reunites with the infinite divine. Love is the only temple that God truly inhabits. Jagadguru Kripalu Ji Maharaj
