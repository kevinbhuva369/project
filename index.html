Divine Selection

MOST POPULAR SACRED TEMPLES

A curated selection of India's most spiritually potent and architecturally significant shrines, visited by millions of seekers annually.

Vrindavan, UP

Prem Mandir

The "Temple of Divine Love" dedicated to Radha Krishna and Sita Ram, carved from Italian white marble.

Vrindavan, UP

Shri Hit Radha Keli Kunj

A sacred sanctuary of the Radhavallabh tradition, where the eternal pastimes of the divine couple are celebrated.

Madurai, TN

Meenakshi Temple

An architectural masterpiece dedicated to Meenakshi, a form of Parvati, and her consort, Sundareshwarar.

Rudraprayag, UK

Kedarnath Temple

One of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Shiva, located in the Garhwal Himalayan range.

Puri, Odisha

Konark Sun Temple

A 13th-century CE Sun temple built attributed to king Narasimhadeva I of the Eastern Ganga Dynasty.

Dwarka, Gujarat

Dwarkadhish Temple

Also known as the Jagat Mandir, dedicated to Krishna, who is worshipped here as the 'King of Dwarka'.

Rudraprayag, Uttarakhand

Trijuginarayan Temple

A sacred site where Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati's celestial wedding took place in the presence of Vishnu.

Veraval, Gujarat

Somnath Temple

The first of the twelve holy Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva, located on the western coast of Gujarat.

Uttarkashi, UK

Gangotri Temple

The highest temple dedicated to Goddess Ganga, marking the spiritual source of the sacred river.

Thanjavur, TN

Thanjavur Brihadeeswara

A masterpiece of Chola architecture. Its 216-foot vimana remains shadowless at noon, an engineering marvel.

Varanasi, UP

Kashi Vishwanath

Standing on the western bank of the holy river Ganga, this is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the holiest of Shiva temples.

Ayodhya, UP

Ram Mandir

The magnificent temple in Ayodhya dedicated to Lord Ram, built with pink sandstone in Nagara style architecture.
